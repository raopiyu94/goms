Small and useful essays

Following are a few examples of short essays you need to write in application if the university you are applying doesnot demand SOP, and instead asks you a few general questions.

Do not copy these essays or your application will be subjected to plagiarism

Are your grades a measure of your performance?

Albert Einstein wrote, “Everybody is a genius. But if you judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree, it will live its whole life believing that it is stupid.” Though I believe my grades are a reflection of my capabilities and hard work, they do not provide accurate information about my personality. Electronics has always been my interest and my grades are a projection of my increasing passion for it as they are constantly increasing bit by bit every semester. Along with my grades I have also been an active participant in various co-curricular competitions conducted in my degree program. Also I participated in a national level project presentation competition. I believe overall performance reflects my personality and my interest in the course of electronics and electrical.

Why XYZ university?

When I was in my secondary school, I started developing interest in physics and mathematics. Later my interest grew in field of electronics, especially robotics and wireless communications when I started developing few projects based on RF. Your esteemed university curriculum offers me modules I love and the research facility of your university is a feather in the hat for me. I believe XYZ University will provide a chance of overall development of the knowledge and passion that I have for electronics.

Important

While going to speaking section, have smile on your face all the time. Greet your examiner first. That will create impression. Don’t argue with examiner just give the answers like usual conversation.

Check list

  • Carry passport, a pen, 2 sharpened pencils and eraser
  • Take a printed copy of appointment letter email.
  • Use only the signature on passport (if you have more than 1).
  • While going for speaking section carry a photocopy of your passport. Examiners might take it for their record.
  • Do not switch to local language or use non English words in speaking section.

VISA Procedures and Protcols

After you receive your I-20 from your university, you now have to start the application process for a student F-1 visa

Before starting the DS-160 form make sure you have the following things handy:

  1. I-20 from your University
  2. Passport
  3. SEVIS fee receipt
  4. Details of your parents
  5. Details of previous visits to international countries
  6. Details of immediate relatives in USA
  7. Details of two people who can vouch for your verification

Steps to books an appointment for VISA interview

  1. First search for DS-160 form on google or go to this link to start the visa application process. https://ceac.state.gov/genniv/
  2. For all F-1 student visas, it is necessary to pay the SEVIS fee or I-901 fee which is $200. The link for SEVIS or I-901 payment is https://fmjfee.com/
  3. You will require to enter your personal details as well as the SEVIS ID which is provided on your I-20.
  4. Before payment, it will allow you to review the information. Then you can proceed with payment either by credit card or by western union/money order.
  5. Once the payment is done, you will get a SEVIS fee receipt. Make sure you save this or print it out.
  6. On opening the link to DS-160 form, you will have to choose the embassy of your choice. Choose the one closest to you.
  7. Next you will have to choose a security question. Make sure you remember the answer because it will help you retrieve your application in case you forget the login credentials.
  8. A new form will be generated and it will also create an application ID. Make a note of this ID since it will be used to login to your application.
  9. Then fill out all the details asked accordingly in the DS-160 form. Make sure you click on save your application at every page. It becomes very tedious otherwise because each page has a timeout. If the time taken to fill that page exceeds that, then you will have to login again and redo the pages that weren’t saved.
  10. After completion of the form, make sure you take a print out of the form or save the form as a pdf. This will help you know later on with the visa interview as to what details you have provided in your application.
  11. To apply for the U.S Visa, search for U.S Visa application on google or go to the link http://www.ustraveldocs.com/in/index.html?firstTime=No
  12. Create an account here by providing your personal details.
  13. You will have to make two appointments: one for your biometrics and one for the visa interview. Choose the date and time for both the appointments.
  14. After making the appointments, pay the visa fees which is $160. Though the visa fees are in dollars, you will have to make the payment in local currency.
  15. For your biometrics, take your DS-160 confirmation page, your appointment confirmation page, passport, I-20, SEVIS fee receipt. You will not be able to carry your cell-phones, bags or any folders. So carry these in your hand.
  16. While scanning the barcode of your appointment page, they will put a sticker on your passport. Make sure not to remove this sticker. This will be scanned at your visa interview.
  17. During your biometrics, they will take your photo and your fingerprint. That completes your biometric session.

TOEFL

Test Pattern

Section 1: Reading (60-100 minutes)

  1. 3 passages (13 questions each)
  2. 3–5 passages (12–14 questions each, 60–100 minutes) IF RESEARCH SECTION IS ALSO INCLUDED.
Section 2: Listening (60-90 minutes)
  1. 4–6 lectures (6 questions each) RESEARCH SECTION IS ALSO INCLUDED.
  2. 2–3 conversations (5 questions each) RESEARCH SECTION IS ALSO INCLUDED.
  3. It will take about 60 minutes to listen to the Conversations and Lectures and to answer the questions. You will have 35 minutes to respond to the questions.
BREAK of 10 minutes
Section 3: Speaking (6 tasks, 20 minutes)
  1. 2 Independent tasks
    • Preparation Time : 15 seconds
    • Response Time : 45 seconds
  2. 4 Integrated tasks
    • Reading + Listening + Speaking (2 tasks)
      • Reading time (45 seconds) then listen to conversation/lecture
      • Preparation Time : 30 seconds
      • Response Time : 60 seconds
    • Listening + Speaking (2 tasks)
      • Listen to conversation/lecture
      • Preparation Time : 20 seconds
      • Response Time : 60 seconds
Section 4: Writing (2 tasks)
  1. Integrated Task - Reading + Listening + Writing (20 minutes)
    • 3 minutes to read the passage then listen to the lecture
    • 20 minutes to plan and write your response (150-225 words)
      NOTE: Passage will be available on screen while writing your response.
    • Independent Task (20 minutes)
      You will write an essay in response to a question that asks you to state, explain, and support your opinion on an issue.
END of the TEST

Before TOEFL

Only change is while booking date for TOEFL you also need to select the 4 universities (sorted by the states they are in) to whom you want to send your score.
Rest all the procedures right from form filling to security checking and allotment of workstation in exam centre are exactly similar to GRE.
Furthermore, you have to adjust the volume of your microphone as per the instructions given by the employee of the exam centre.
Try to keep volume bit on the higher side because many people will have speaking sections together which might make you inaudible if you have a soft voice.
There are no formalities after completing the test
